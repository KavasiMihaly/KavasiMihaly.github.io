The blog posts will generally fit into one or more of these categories

Big Picture

These posts will talk about how self-service BI can help your organization, what are the many obstacles or how to go through the journey to become data-driven.

Analytics Edge

These posts will cover specific capabilities, ideas to solve certain analytics requirements. Mostly focusing the report building and calculation aspects.

Improve the Experience

These posts will try to cover how you can improve the experience for the consumers of your reports and dashboards.

Get to the data

These posts will cover all the challenges that arise when we want to connect and prepare various data sources.
